DTG 2024

Participation in Dhaka

  • Texcorp Trading Limited showcased cutting-edge technology at the Dhaka International Textile & Garments Machinery Exhibition 2024, where they represented esteemed brands Kaeser Compressor and Uster Technologies. With a commitment to excellence and innovation, Texcorp demonstrated the latest advancements in compressor and quality control technologies. Through their participation, they aimed to not only highlight the capabilities of their represented brands but also to foster collaborations and partnerships within the textile and garments industry. Texcorp's presence at the exhibition underscored their dedication to providing top-tier solutions to meet the evolving needs of the textile sector, further solidifying their position as a leading provider of industrial equipment and technology in Bangladesh and beyond.
